# Extensor Indicus

Action: Extends Digit II
Extra Info: - Accessory Extensor of Digit II (Pointer Finger)
Innervation: Radial Nerve and Posterior Interosseous Nerve (C7 and C8 but predominantly C7)
Insertion: Extensor Hood of Digit II
Origin: Posterior Ulna and Interosseous Membrane
